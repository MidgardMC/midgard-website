Update Aquatic Information

To our members

Minecraft Update Aquatic has been one of the most anticipated releases in the game's history. To that end, we've been working dilligently to ensure a smooth transition from our old map, to our new permanent home.


Some things of note:

  • As of 10/23/2018, we are currently laying the groundwork for the launch of the new map, which includes building out a suitable spawn area and cardinal roads so players can start exploring.
  • We are currently working on planning out how chests and builds from the 1.12 map will be transferred over. Please be patient with us as we work through these issues.

We've made great strides in getting spawn set up and the roads built, so it won't be too much longer. Stay tuned to this website and our Discord server for the latest updates.

We thank you for your patience.


— Midgard Staff
