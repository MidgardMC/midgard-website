Get to know us.

Midgard is an enhanced vanilla whitelist survival server with heavy emphasis on community. We have been around for over half a decade and we pride ourselves on providing our players with a fast, large server to play on. While we cater to the mature, seasoned player, our server is family-friendly.

Meet the team.

Midgard is staffed by seasoned pros in the Minecraft space. With IT Professionals running the back end. And veteran players staffed in game. Midgard will make sure you're in good hands.
