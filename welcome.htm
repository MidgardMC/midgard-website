Welcome Gamealition Members!

To all of our Gamealition friends

When the announcement of Gamealition's closure was made, we started the process of making your transition as easy as possible. This includes the relocation of builds across to the Midgard map, so you may continue to enjoy them.


A couple of conditions

  • The build needs to be able to be relocated using WorldEdit — This means a complete structure in some cube shape that will fit in WorldEdit, we can split them if necessary but the new location needs to be large enough to accept the build being moved.
  • We will do our best to get your complete build copied, but do understand that certain elements and parts may not be able to be copied.

Chests and enchanted tools

Due to results discovered in testing, we have found that enchanted items coming from a different server will drop their enchants and/or cause corruption on the destination map — items lose all durability and drop their enchants. Therefore, we will not be moving chests with enchanted items.

When we adapted our build move policy for those coming or returning from Gamealition, it is to preserve builds, and not to give players a shortcut in mining and enchanting items on the new server. It should only take a week or two of occasional play to mine materials and enchant tools to serve as replacements. You are welcome to use any public grinder and enchanting tables to speed things up, and all players start out with $1,000 of in-game currency. You may choose to purchase items including enchanted tools from the many shops available on Midgard.

This is purely to keep things fair for all players and to prevent corruption of transferred items.

Thank you for reading and understanding — if you have any questions or concerns, please message any of our moderators or admins in-game or on Discord.

Welcome to Midgard! All players coming from Gamealition are automatically upgraded to full-player status. We sincerely hope you enjoy playing here, as we plan to be here for a long time to come!

— Midgard Staff
